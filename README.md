## 🚀 Featured Project — BostonHome.app

<div align="center">

### 🏠 [bostonhome.app](https://www.bostonhome.app)
**Community-Driven Rent Reporting for the Greater Boston Region**

</div>

A civic tech platform empowering Boston renters with **transparent, community-sourced rental data** across the Greater Boston area. In a city where rent transparency is scarce and negotiating power is unequal, BostonHome gives renters the information they need.

**Key Features:**
- 📍 Neighborhood-level rent data reported by real tenants
- 📊 Trend tracking across the Greater Boston housing market
- 🤝 Community-first, privacy-respecting data model
- 🔍 Searchable by unit type, neighborhood, and price range

> *Built for Boston renters, by someone who lives here.*
